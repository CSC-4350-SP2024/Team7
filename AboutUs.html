About EggCrypt

With EggCrypt, password systems are approached in a whole new way that combines user engagement and security. Users are sent to an entertaining and secure environment when they choose an image from our website. The selected image is transformed, breaking up into nine disorganized fragments, giving users an engaging challenge to solve. By navigating through the disorganized components, users actively contribute to strengthening their own digital security in addition to gaining access. EggCrypt aims to transform the way people engage with password systems, making security both user-friendly and entertaining, rather than only protecting data.
